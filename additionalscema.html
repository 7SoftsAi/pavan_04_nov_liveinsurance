Additional Tax Advantage Schemes

4.1 Tax Saving Fixed Deposits

  • Eligibility: Individuals and Hindu Undivided Families (HUF)
  • Investment Limit: Up to INR 1.5 Lakhs per annum
  • Tax Benefit: Under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act
  • Tenure: 5 years
  • Interest Rates: Vary based on the bank or financial institution
  • Premature Withdrawal: Penalty applicable
  • Additional Benefits: Loan against FD, Quarterly/Monthly interest payouts

4.2 Equity-Linked Savings Schemes (ELSS)

  • Eligibility: Individuals and HUF
  • Investment Limit: Up to INR 1.5 Lakhs per annum
  • Tax Benefit: Under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act
  • Lock-in Period: 3 years
  • Investment Type: Mutual Funds investing primarily in equities
  • Risk Factor: Market-linked, high risk compared to fixed deposits
  • Returns: Potentially higher returns due to equity exposure

4.3 National Pension System (NPS)

  • Eligibility: All Indian citizens aged between 18 and 65 years
  • Investment Limit: Up to INR 1.5 Lakhs per annum under Section 80C, additional INR 50,000 under Section 80CCD(1B)
  • Tax Benefit: Under Section 80C and Section 80CCD(1B)
  • Tenure: Long-term investment with partial withdrawals allowed
  • Returns: Market-linked, varying based on fund performance
  • Benefits: Regular pension after retirement, tax-free accumulation
  • Flexibility: Various investment options and fund choices

4.4 Life Insurance Policies

  • Eligibility: Individuals, HUF
  • Investment Limit: No limit on premiums paid, tax benefit up to INR 1.5 Lakhs per annum
  • Tax Benefit: Under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act
  • Policy Tenure: Varies as per policy terms
  • Premium Payment: Regular or single premium options
  • Benefits: Death benefit, maturity benefit, loan against policy
  • Additional Covers: Riders available for enhanced protection
